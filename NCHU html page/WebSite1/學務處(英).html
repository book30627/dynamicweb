Office of Student Affairs

Introduction

The Office of Student Affairs (OSA) is committed to holistic education to prepare students for their future career development in a diverse and global society. The OSA comprises 7 execution units, including the Office of Military Education, the Division of Student Life, the Division of Extracurricular Activities, the Division of Student Housing Service, the Division of Overseas Chinese Student Affairs, the Career Development Center and the Health and Counseling Center. The OSA objective is to foster a healthy, safe, and supportive learning environment outside the classroom for students to pursue extracurricular success, autonomous learning, and social enrichment.
