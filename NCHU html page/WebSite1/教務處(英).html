Introduction

  • Supervision of the executions of projects undertaken by different divisions of the Office of Academic Affairs and the implementations of tasks undertaken by the Center for General Education

  • Statistical analysis and collation of all kinds of data regarding academic affairs
  • Dispatch, registration and filing of the official documents for all the divisions under the Office of Academic Affairs
