COLLEGE OF LAW AND POLITICS

Introduction

The College of Law and Politics was set up in 2000 when the College of Social Science and Management was established. This College aims at specialized support, educational structures and training to meet social needs. Founded in 2011, the college consists of one department and three graduate institutes, which comes from the College of Social Science and Management. And the college has excellence in teaching and research and links with professionals, the industry and non-governmental organizations. The purpose is to increase a wide range of knowledge and understanding in the domain of law, politics, public affairs and professional education so as to equip graduates to make contribution to society for their future careers. Also, The core values of the College are to pursue excellence in research and teaching.

The College offers bachelor's, master's and doctoral programs for local and international students. As of 2012, there are about 630 students at this College, including 220 undergraduates, 400 postgraduates and 8 international students.
