COLLEGE OF SCIENCE

Introduction

The College of Science consists of the Departments of Chemistry, Applied Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science and Engineering. Every Department offers BS, MS and PhD programs. The College caters for about 1,000 undergraduate, 500 MS and 250 PhD students. There are 99 fulltime faculty members of the College who are all specialized in their particular fields. The College is striving to raise the faculty member/student ratio to further enhance teaching and advance research. Every year, NSC sponsors about 80 projects with a total grant support of around NT $100,000,000..
